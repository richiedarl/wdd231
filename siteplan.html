Awka Chamber of Commerce

WDD 231 Website Project – Site Plan

Site Name

Awka Chamber of Commerce

The name clearly represents a professional business organization serving Awka. It establishes local identity, credibility, and recognition for businesses operating within the region.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Awka Chamber of Commerce website is to provide a central platform where local businesses can connect, gain visibility, and access chamber services. The site will include a business directory, membership application form, community event highlights, and spotlight promotions.

Scenarios

  • How can I apply to become a member of the Awka Chamber of Commerce?
  • Where can I find contact information for local businesses in Awka?

Color Scheme

  • #333333 (Primary): Used for header, footer, and navigation areas.
  • #e6e6e6 (Secondary): Used for background and section separation.
  • #004aad (Accent Blue): Used for headings and call-to-action highlights.

Typography

  • Roboto: Used for all headings and body text to maintain modern readability and consistency.
